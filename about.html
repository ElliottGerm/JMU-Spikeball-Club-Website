About the Club

The JMU Spikeball Club is a recreational sports club. We do not have tryouts and we invite all skill levels to join. We host a local spikeball tournament at U-park once per semester and of course we play pick up games multiple times a week. For all those interested you can usually find us at Hillside in front of Madison Union.
